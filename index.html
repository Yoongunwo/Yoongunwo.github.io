Geonwo Yoon


I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Pusan National University and am currently pursuing my M.S. at S3Lab under the supervision of Prof. Yoon-Ho Choi. My research interests include event collection and security in distributed networked systems and cloud-native environments, with a focus on eBPF-based runtime monitoring and ML-based anomaly detection.
